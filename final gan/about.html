About Us

New definition of transportation in rural Haryana Dear friends, We all know that transportation problems always remain a big challenge in the villages of Haryana. Addressing this problem, our efforts have a new name – 'GANTAVYA'. 'GANTAVYA' means transport services with social, partnership, and environmental excellence as gateways to prosperity. This new definition is taking us toward the development of a safe, accessible, and sensitive transportation system. Under 'GANTAVYA', we will not only give priority to affordable and effective transport facilities, but we will also involve the people of villages through digital platforms. With this, we will not only open the doors of development but will also promote village self-reliance. Evoking the 'Destination', we invite you all to support and partner with this project. We all will work together to make this new definition of transportation in villages successful. GANTAVYA, a visionary RURAL initiative in the travel sector, aims to empower rural communities by harnessing the untapped potential of their unique landscapes. With a commitment to sustainable development and community involvement, GANTAVYA seeks to transform rural areas into vibrant travel destinations, fostering economic growth while preserving the authenticity of local life.
